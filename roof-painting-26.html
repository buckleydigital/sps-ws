How It Works

Our Simple
4-Step Process

No shortcuts. No subcontractors. Just a clean, professional finish every time.

01
Roof Wash

We start with a thorough wash to remove dirt, moss, lichen, and all surface contaminants, ensuring the paint bonds perfectly.

02
Prime & Seal

Any cracked or damaged areas are repaired and a quality primer is applied to lock in the surface and ensure long-lasting adhesion.

03
Premium Paint

We apply high-grade roof paint in your chosen colour, a factory-fresh finish that protects against UV, rain, and heat for years to come.

04
Final Inspection

Our team does a thorough walkover with you to make sure you're 100% happy. No job is done until you're satisfied.
