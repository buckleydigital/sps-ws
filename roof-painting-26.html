How It Works

Our Simple
2-Step Process

No shortcuts. No subcontractors. Just a clean, professional finish every time.

01

High-Pressure Wash

We start with a thorough high-pressure wash to strip away dirt, moss, lichen, and all surface contaminants — ensuring the paint bonds perfectly.

02

Prime & Seal

Any cracked or damaged areas are repaired and a quality primer is applied to lock in the surface and ensure long-lasting adhesion.

03

Premium Paint

We apply high-grade roof paint in your chosen colour — delivering a factory-fresh finish that protects against UV, rain and heat for years to come.

04

Final Inspection

Our team does a thorough walkover with you to make sure you're 100% happy with the result. No job is done until you're satisfied.
